Salient Features of
VLE

  • In VLE, Learning materials are organized in folders for easy navigation

  • Asynchronous learning is supported via the availability of all material on VLE including audios, videos, documents, etc, 24/7

  • Access to material can be linked to the completion of milestones

  • Online quizzes, on VLE, can offer immediate feedback and scoring

  • Teachers can allow multiple attempts at quizzes and assignments

  • Discussion forums can work well but need to be carefully planned, well promoted, and managed.

  • You need to clearly define the purpose of the discussion forum to your learners and link it to face-to-face activities

  • To support a variety of learning materials, a vast variety of file formats are supported

  • Interactive activities in the form of quizzes, assignments, discussion forums Wiki tools, are integral part of our VLE

  • Collaborative assignments can be set by the teacher in a wiki-like environ-ment to encourage collaboration among students

  • For example, you might want to encourage learners to use a discussion forum for FAQs or course queries instead of emailing the tutor.

  • This means that all learners see the responses and encourages more independent learning.
